Share your medical records

You can choose parts of your medical records and share them with the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP).

This could help you to get a faster and more accurate decision.

It might mean you don’t need a face to face appointment with an assessor.

Anything you choose to share will only be used for your Employment and Support Allowance claim.

Your information won’t be used for anything else, or shared with anyone else.
