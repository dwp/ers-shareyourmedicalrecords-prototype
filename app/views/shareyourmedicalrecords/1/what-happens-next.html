What happens next

Your medical information will be looked at by a healthcare professional who is trained to see whether you meet the criteria to get ESA.

DWP might be able to make a decision based on this information. If not, you’ll need to go to a face to face appointment to assess whether:

  • you need some help before you can work again
  • you can’t work, and probably can’t work for a long time
  • you’re able to work now

Within the next 2 weeks you’ll receive either:

  • a decision
  • an appointment for a face to face assessment
