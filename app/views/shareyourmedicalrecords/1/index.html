ESA - share you medical records

About this prototype

This prototype is an in-browser prototype.

It was made with the intention of being used as a stimulus in a research session to see:

  • if citizens would be comfortable with being told about the service by text message
  • what forms of logging into a service citizens would be comfortable using
  • if citizens would be comfortable having the medical record held by their GP imported into such a service
  • if citizens would be comfortable selecting what they want to share (and restrict beinbg shared, by not being selected)
